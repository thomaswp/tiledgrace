Step 2: Make Alonzo move to a random spot when clicked

This task involves adding blocks to your step 1 solution so that, every time Alonzo is clicked, he moves to a random position (between -190 and 190 in the x direction, and between -130 and 130 in the y direction).

This is the first time you'll be using blocks that are shaped like ovals. This means these blocks are used to return values and not to do anything, and they are meant to be dragged into other oval inputs, which will turn white to indicate you can drop them in.

Remember, to find out what a block does, hover over it's name.
