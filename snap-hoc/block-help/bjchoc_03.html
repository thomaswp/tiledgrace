Step 4: Make Alonzo move by itself if it's not clicked in time

In this step, you'll implement a feature that will really make the game feel like "whack-a-mole" -- you want to have Alonzo move by himself to a new random position every 2 seconds.

Right now, whenever Alonzo is clicked he only turns and moves once, but we want him to keep moving. We can do that by putting those two commands inside of a forever block. You may have to drag them out of the whenClicked block first, put them into a forever block and then move them back. Of course, Alonzo is pretty fast, so we need to tell it to wait( ) after every move, to give the player a chance to click on it.

If Alonzo gets carried away, remember you can use the Stop button at the bottom of the screen to stop it's movement.
